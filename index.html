HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.io.

Get some SOL

have SOL in your wallet to switch to $GEICO. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Jup.ag

connect to Jup. Go to jup.ag in google chrome or on the browser inside your wallet app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $GEICO token address into Jup.ag, select GEICO, and confirm. When wallet prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for GEICO

switch SOL for $GEICO. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
